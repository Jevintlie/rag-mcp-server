Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Actuarial Studies

Overview

This programme draws together a variety of subjects that involve applying mathematical and statistical techniques in the financial services industry. Designed to pave the way for students who want to enter a career as an actuary, they will be prepared for professional exams that will lead to a recognised actuary status or a specialist in the financial risk and insurance industries.

The Society of Actuaries (SOA) USA, Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA) UK, Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) USA, The Institute of Actuaries of Australia (IAA), and Canadian Institute of Actuaries (CIA) conduct actuarial professional exams leading to professional designations such as Fellow of the Society of Actuaries (FSA), Fellow of the Institute of Actuaries (FIA), Chartered Enterprise Risk Analyst (CERA), Fellow of the Casualty Actuarial Society (FCAS), Fellow of the Institute of Actuaries of Australia (FIAA) and Fellow of the Canadian Institute of Actuaries (FCIA).

Professional Pathways & Exemptions

Image

IFoA

Our programme is accredited by the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA), UK. Students in our programme are eligible for exemptions from 6 IFoA papers, which are: CS1, CS2, CM1, CM2, CB1, and CB2.

Image

SOA

Our program is also recognized by the Society of Actuaries (SOA) and listed in SOA Universities & Colleges with Actuarial Programs - Advanced Curriculum (UCAP-AC). We cover six preliminary exams in the curriculum and provide three VEE exemptions. 

Image

CAS

Our programme has been recognized by the Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) as a Silver Level partner. We offer students comprehensive preparation for the CAS exams and a career in general insurance. Our students have the opportunity to earn up to five exam credits and we also equip them to take additional exams such as Exam 5 and MAS-II.

For more info, please visit: https://www.casact.org/

Image

Note: Eligibility of exemptions is determined based on subjects taken and grades obtained.

Actuarial Practice Course (APC)

 

Image
Image

Actuarial Practice Course (APC) is conducted by our in-house actuary, Ms Sophia Chng, Fellow of the Institute & Faculty of Actuaries (UK).

Get ahead with this 3-month intensive hands-on course designed to prepare actuarial students to be employment-ready. It incorporates feedback from our practicing actuaries and industry practitioners in the course design. This course is conducted by our in-house actuary - Ms Sophia Chng, focusing on current topics in the insurance industry.

In addition to the standard university subjects and assignments, students have the option to enrol in this course, offered exclusively to Sunway Universitys actuarial students without additional charges.

We are proud to have a track record of 100%* employment in actuarial-related positions for students who successfully completed this APC.

*Based on School of Mathematical Sciences graduate tracer study
